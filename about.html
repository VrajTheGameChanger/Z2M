About Rural Maker Fest

RuralMaker Fest 2018 Idar Days & Dates: March 4 & 5, 2018 Sunday& Monday Time: 10 am to 5 pm. Venue: UniqYOU International School, Near Bus Stand, Jawanpura, Idar Objectives: • To enable and facilitate rural and tribal students of standard 5 and higher for the North Gujarat in particular and for Gujarat overall… • To ignite students to start thinking about things. Start thinking innovatively. • To lead students to think & explore the possible pain points or challenges around them at home, school, society etc. • To become maker (From Zero to Maker) and thus help them develop creative thinking and problem solving – two of the crucial 21st century skills. About My School

India vision 2020 is a plan proposed by Dr. Abdul Kalam to make India a developed country by 2020; An action plan to develop India into a knowledge super power. To achieve this goal, ‘PURA’ model has been designed to provide urban amenities in rural areas. I would like to brief you about UniqYOU international school. This school is conducted by The good Human being foundation, Gandhinagar, is the premier education institution in the suburbs with an excellent recording in academic and extracurricular activities. It is a nonsectarian in character providing equal facilities for admission to students and irrespective of caste or cribs. It pay careful attention to physical , mental and moral education with a view to build up a healthy personality in inculcating the principles of self-reliance, self-discipline and independent thinking in order to lay the foundation of good citizenship. Our goal is to bring up the level of education higher in the village by providing them equal level of education in the city. Our founders of school Prof. Jitendra Patel and Dr.Yogesh Patel, who belongs from rural village, but their thinking and hard work made the school to reach the goal up to International level.
